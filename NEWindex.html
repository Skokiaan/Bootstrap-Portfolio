About Me

I was born quite young for my age. I grew in direct portion to the quantity of nurishment I received from my parents.

A bunch of other things happened over the years. I won't go into detail. I just need to keep typing to have more text on this page. A little more should do it. There we go.

My ultimate goal is to stave off death until the very last moments of my life.
